Dasha & Andrew

Who we are

We are a happy family, addicted to photography, and making a world a better place.

The truth of the matter is that art, beauty and happiness are all around us. And one cannot resist the feeling of raising a camera, framing a shot, and capturing that moment into eternity.

Our Story

Dasha is a natural artist and has a way of capturing the most atmospheric photo , the artist eye also helps her capture the soul of the event and really zero in on the required mood and colors. And Andrew has his own vision of zany and unique event moments which seals the deal and guarantees coverage from all angles.

Our Inspiration

Every shot we take is driven by the feelings and emotions you or the final user is going to experience. For some it will be a precious memory, a wow effect for others, or maybe a satisfaction for their product presented in the best light.
